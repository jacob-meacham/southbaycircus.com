Who are you?
South Bay Circus Arts (SBCA) is a gathering of circus artists from many disciplines who have come together to bring circus arts classes, training opportunities, and performances to the South Bay. We also sponsor a Meetup to facilitate informal circus arts jam sessions (note: unless otherwise specified, SBCA does not host and is not responsible for events organized through the Meetup).
How can I join?
To find a class, see the Classes schedule. If you want to learn a skill but do not currently see a class offered in it, contact us. We are happy to expand our class offerings if we know there is demand. You can also contact us to arrange private lessons.
Do I need any circus experience?
Not at all! We offer a multitude of beginner classes in all circus arts. If you're not sure whether a skill is right for you, the broader community down here can give you a few pointers before you start your first class. Find them on Meetup.
what do I need to bring to class?
Curiosity, respect for your instructor and fellow students, and a willingness to try!
You guys rock! Are you looking for teachers/performers?
Definitely! If you've got a cool skill that you'd like to share, or are passionate about teaching, drop us a line over at our contact page.
